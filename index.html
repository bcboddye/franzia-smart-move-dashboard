☀️ Summer is the Efficiency King

The Summer flight delivered the highest Incremental ROAS at $5.11 — 2.6× better than Holiday. With a $162K investment, it generated $828K in incremental sales plus a projected $1.32M 12-month new buyer return. Prioritize summer budgets for peak efficiency.

🎄 Holiday Wins the New Buyer Battle

Despite a lower iROAS of $1.97, Holiday drove the highest new buyer rate at 31% — over 2× Q1's rate of 15%. The season pulls in trial shoppers who may convert to loyal buyers. Holiday spend is a brand equity & acquisition play.

🌸 Q1 FY26 — The Scale Monster

The Spring campaign achieved the highest total ROAS at $56.30 and drove $9.1M in total sales with the widest audience reach (18.3M unique viewers). At an iROAS of $4.16, it's a reliable efficiency driver for Q1 planning with loyal Franzia audiences.
